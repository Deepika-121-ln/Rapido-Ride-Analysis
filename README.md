# 🚖 Rapido Ride Booking Data Analytics Using SQL and Power BI

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Rapido ride booking data to uncover valuable business insights related to bookings, revenue, customer behavior, ride cancellations, payment methods, and vehicle performance.

Using SQL for data analysis and Power BI for visualization, the project demonstrates an end-to-end analytics workflow that supports data-driven decision-making.

## Objectives

- Analyze ride booking trends.
- Measure overall business performance.
- Track booking completion and cancellation rates.
- Compare Bike and Auto ride performance.
- Understand customer payment preferences.
- Evaluate customer satisfaction using ratings.
- Generate insights to support business decisions.

The dataset contains ride booking information including:

- Booking ID

- Booking Value

- Cancellation Details

## Business Problems

### Booking Analysis

- How many rides were completed successfully?
- What is the average ride duration?
- What is the average ride distance for each vehicle type?
- How many rides were canceled by customers?

### Customer Analysis

- Which vehicle type receives better customer ratings?
- Who are the top 5 customers based on total bookings?

### Revenue Analysis

- Which payment methods are most commonly used?
- How much revenue was generated by Bike and Auto rides?

### Performance Analysis

- What is the average ride time for each vehicle type?
- What is the most common reason for incomplete rides?

## Findings

- **Booking Trends:** Most ride bookings were completed successfully, while cancelled and incomplete rides highlighted opportunities to improve service efficiency.
- **Revenue Growth:** Bike and Auto services both contributed significantly to overall revenue, reflecting consistent business performance.
- **Customer Satisfaction:** Customer ratings remained positive across vehicle types, indicating a reliable and satisfactory ride experience.
- **Peak Hours:** Ride demand increased during specific time periods, highlighting the importance of efficient driver allocation.

## Conclusion

The analysis reveals that operational efficiency can be improved by:

- Reducing ride cancellations through better driver availability.
- Optimizing driver allocation during peak demand periods.
- Enhancing customer experience by maintaining high service quality.
- Leveraging booking and payment insights to support business growth.
